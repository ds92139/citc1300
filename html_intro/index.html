Less Common Instruments

Balafon

A West African instrument that's similar to a marimba, but with it's own distinct sound and shape.

Dizi

Also called the Chinese bamboo flute, this instrument works like a flute but with more of a bright or sharper sound quality to it.

Jaw Harp

This instrument is played using the mouth, and it shows up in various cultures around the world with minor differences.

Performances for the instruments:

  • Balafon
  • Dizi
  • Jaw Harp
  • Bonus: Balafon with non-Western tuning
