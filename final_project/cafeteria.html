the cafeteria

The cafeteria is one of the most popular areas on campus, making it a prime spot during usual lunchtime(11-12 PM). Many different types of food and drink are available for students to get, whether a worker cooks something off the grill for them or they grab a pre-made food from the hot bar. Other options are available too, like the specials bar that alternates each day between many foods. There's also the snack bar across the room as well. One of the most notable parts of the cafeteria is its convenience. Not only are there vending machines there, but also it's in OMNI, making it close to many classrooms.
